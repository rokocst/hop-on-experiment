# Hop On - Social Media Ad Copy Variants

## Discord/WhatsApp Message Variants (A/B Test These)

### Variant A: "Guaranteed Outlet" (Utility-Focused)
```
📚 Library full again?

We built a live map showing which cafés near campus have open outlets RIGHT NOW.

✅ Monigram: 2 outlets open
❌ Balzac's: Full
✅ Smile Tiger: 5+ outlets

Check availability + get notified when spots open:
👉 [hop-on.link/live]

(Testing this during midterms week - would you use this?)
```

**Rationale:** Emphasizes the core utility — outlet certainty. Direct, problem-solution framing.

---

### Variant B: "Quiet Space" (Study Environment-Focused)
```
Tired of studying in the hallway? 🏢

Find quiet cafés near campus with available seating + outlets.

Live status for:
• Monigram Coffee
• Balzac's
• Smile Tiger

Get notified when your fav spot has space:
👉 [hop-on.link/live]

(Piloting this week — feedback welcome!)
```

**Rationale:** Emphasizes study environment quality, not just outlets. Tests if "quiet" resonates more than "outlet."

---

### Variant C: "Peak Hours" (Time-Sensitive Urgency)
```
⏰ 11 AM - 2 PM = café chaos

Avoid the "no outlets left" walk of shame.

Check live availability at 3 cafés before you go:
🔌 Monigram: 2 outlets
🔌 Balzac's: 0 outlets  
🔌 Smile Tiger: 5+ outlets

Reserve your spot:
👉 [hop-on.link/live]
```

**Rationale:** Creates urgency around peak study hours. Tests if time-based FOMO drives more clicks.

## Testing Strategy

**Day 1 (Tuesday):**
- Post Variant A in Conestoga Discord #general at 10:30 AM
- Post Variant C in WhatsApp study group at 11:15 AM

**Day 2 (Wednesday):**
- Post Variant B in Discord #study-spots at 10:30 AM
- Repost Variant A in different WhatsApp group at 12:00 PM

**Track:**
- Which variant gets more QR scans (use bit.ly links to differentiate)
- Which gets more email sign-ups
- Time of day with highest engagement

## Follow-Up Message (If Someone Asks Questions)

```
Hey! This is a pilot project for a midterms experiment.

The idea: Instead of walking to 3 cafés hoping one has an outlet, you can check availability from your phone.

Right now it's manual (we update every 30 min), but if enough people use it, we'll automate it.

Would you actually use this during finals week?
```

---

## Metrics to Track

For your assignment report, log:
1. **Impressions**: # of people who saw the message (estimate based on Discord/WhatsApp member count)
2. **Clicks**: # of unique QR scans or link clicks (use bit.ly analytics)
3. **Conversions**: # of email sign-ups
4. **Time of Day**: Peak engagement hours
5. **Message Variant Performance**: Which copy got the most clicks

---

## Pro Tips

- Post during peak procrastination hours (10-11 AM, 7-9 PM)
- Pin the message in Discord if mods allow
- Follow up in comments: "Just checked — Smile Tiger still has 3 spots open 👀"
- Create FOMO: "12 people signed up in the last hour 🔥"
